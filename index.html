How to pretzel and ingredients

ingredients

Dough
  • 250 g white flour
  • 1/2 packet of dry yeast
  • 5 g salt
  • 70 ml oat milk
  • 70 ml of water
  • 20 g (rapeseed) oil

Lye

  • 1 liter of water
  • 100 g of baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • coarse sea salt

preparation

  1. Mix the dry ingredients, then add the liquid ingredients and knead for 5-10 minutes to form a uniform dough. Cover and leave to rise for 40 minutes until doubled in size.
  2. Divide the dough into 3 pieces, roll out each piece with the palms of your hands into 2 strands about 20-50 cm (~40 g) long. The ends should be much thinner than the centre.
  3. To make a heart-shaped pretzel, take two ‘snakes’ and wrap the ends around each other twice. Then pull them apart towards the outside and wrap the other two ends together to form the pointed base of the heart.
  4. Place the pretzel on baking paper on a cutting board and place in the refrigerator for 20 minutes.
  5. Preheat the oven to 180° C fan. Bring the water to the boil, dissolve the bicarbonate of soda and salt in it, remove the pan from the heat. Using a slotted spoon/spatula, dip the pretzels into the warm solution for 20 seconds, drain and place on a baking tray lined with baking paper.
  6. Sprinkle coarse sea salt over the pretzel. Bake for 17 minutes on the bottom shelf of the oven. If necessary, swap the baking tray once from the back to the front so that they brown evenly. Leave to cool slightly.
